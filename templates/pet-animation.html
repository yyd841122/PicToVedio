Pet Animation FAQ

What pet photos work best?

Use clear front-facing cat, dog, or pet portraits with visible eyes and good lighting.

Can pet shops use it?

Yes, pet stores can create short social clips from product, grooming, or adoption photos they own.

What motion should I choose?

Subtle blinking, tiny head turns, and slow camera push-ins usually look cleaner than big body movement.

AI Kiss Video FAQ

What photo works best?

Use a clear couple photo with both faces visible, natural lighting, and no heavy blur or occlusion.

Can I use it commercially?

You can use generated videos if you own the photo rights and follow the platform rules where you publish.

Why can faces distort?

AI video can change details when motion is too strong. Short, gentle prompts usually produce more stable results.

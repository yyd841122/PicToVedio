# FrameVela AI Brand Rename 2026-06-13

## Decision

The owner approved changing the public product name from `MotionPic AI` to `FrameVela AI` on 2026-06-13.

The change was made before completing Creem KYC/KYB because an existing same-category product uses the name [`MotionPic AI Video Generator`](https://mwm.ai/apps/motionpic-ai/6756036730). The owner therefore could not confidently accept Creem's mandatory representation that the product name creates no likely confusion.

## Preliminary Name Check

Exact-name web, software, app, and indexed app-store searches found no result for `FrameVela` or `FrameVela AI` on 2026-06-13.

This is a practical collision check, not a legal trademark opinion or a guarantee that no unindexed or pending registration exists.

## Compatibility Boundary

Public names, SEO metadata, policies, current operating documents, and dashboard labels use `FrameVela AI`.

The following internal identifiers remain unchanged to avoid breaking existing accounts, credits, analytics, webhooks, or database integration:

- Browser storage keys beginning with `motionpic-`.
- `X-MotionPic-User-ID`.
- Server cookies and internal admin headers.
- Supabase RPC and SQL identifiers.
- Existing Render service and repository identifiers.
- Historical reports and payment records created under the former name.

## Remaining Owner Actions

- Rename both Creem test products to `FrameVela AI Creator Pack` and `FrameVela AI Commerce Pack`.
- Keep prices and grants aligned at `$9 / 40 credits` and `$29 / 160 credits`.
- Update the Gmail sender display name to `FrameVela AI Support`.
- Replace the legacy M favicon and PWA icon set when approved FrameVela artwork is available.
- Resume Creem KYC/KYB only after the public deployment and Creem test-product names match.

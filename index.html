Frequently Asked Questions

These answers help visitors understand MotionPic AI, credits, watermark exports, and commercial usage.

What is an AI photo to video generator?

It is an online tool that combines one uploaded photo, a motion prompt, and a template to create a short AI video.

What photos work best?

Portraits, couple photos, pet photos, product shots, model images, and old family photos are strong starting points.

Will generated videos have a watermark?

Free previews are useful for checking the result. HD no-watermark exports use credits or a paid pack.

How do credits work?

Credits are used for previews, HD no-watermark exports, and batch generation. A standard 4-second 720p clip uses 2 credits.

Can I use videos commercially?

The Commerce Pack is designed for product video templates and commercial export workflows for stores and ads.

How is it different from general AI video tools?

General tools are open-ended. MotionPic AI focuses on photo-to-video templates so outputs are closer to ready-to-publish assets.

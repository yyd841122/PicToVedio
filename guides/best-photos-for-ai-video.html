Best Photos for AI Video Generation

The photo matters as much as the model. Better inputs make short AI videos more stable, more natural, and easier to publish.

Upload a Photo

Photo Checklist

Sharp subject

Avoid heavy blur, tiny faces, blocked eyes, or cropped product edges.

Simple background

Clean backgrounds reduce strange motion around hair, hands, pets, and product outlines.

Natural lighting

Even lighting helps preserve identity, skin tone, texture, and product detail.

Best Use Cases

Portraits and couples

Use clear front-facing photos and gentle facial motion prompts.

Products

Use a clean object photo or lifestyle shot where the product is the obvious focus.

Pets and old photos

Use high-resolution scans or photos with visible eyes and a stable pose.

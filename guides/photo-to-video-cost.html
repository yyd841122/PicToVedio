AI Photo-to-Video Cost Guide

AI video generation has real provider cost. A good product flow should help users test quickly, avoid waste, and only spend more credits when a result is worth exporting.

See MotionPic AI Credits

What Drives Cost

Duration

Longer videos require more model compute. Start with short clips for testing.

Resolution

HD and no-watermark exports should be reserved for results you plan to publish.

Retries

Unclear photos or aggressive prompts can force users to regenerate, increasing cost.

How MotionPic AI Keeps It Practical

Short preview first

The default flow favors quick previews before paid exports.

Silent by default

Audio is not generated by default because many social clips use platform music or captions.

Credits instead of subscriptions

Creators can buy only what they need while testing content ideas.

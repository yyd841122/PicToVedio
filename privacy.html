Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

MotionPic AI helps users turn uploaded photos into short AI videos. This policy explains what information we process and how we use it.

Information We Process

  • Uploaded photos and prompts used to create video generation jobs.
  • Generated video URLs, job status, credit usage, and basic account identifiers.
  • Payment events from Creem, including product, plan, transaction identifiers, and credit amounts.
  • Technical information such as browser-local anonymous user IDs, request logs, errors, and device/browser data.

How We Use Information

  • To generate, preview, and deliver AI video results.
  • To maintain credit balances and prevent duplicate payment credits.
  • To troubleshoot failed jobs, improve templates, and protect the service from abuse.
  • To respond to support requests and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Uploaded Photos And Generated Videos

Uploaded photos and generated videos may be sent to third-party AI infrastructure providers only for the purpose of processing the requested job. Do not upload photos unless you have the right to use them.

Payments

Payments are processed by Creem. MotionPic AI does not store full card numbers. We receive payment confirmation events so credits can be added to the correct account.

Data Retention

We keep account, credit, payment, and generation records as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and improve reliability. Future storage settings may allow users to delete generated assets.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@cozyguidehub.com.
